# SaaS Churn & Revenue Risk Analysis

## Objective
Analyze customer churn drivers and quantify revenue at risk across subscription plans and acquisition channels.

## Tools Used
- SQL
- Excel
- Tableau

## Dataset
Synthetic SaaS subscription dataset containing customer plans, tenure, acquisition channels, churn status, and lifetime revenue.

## Key Business Questions
- Which subscription plans experience the highest churn?
- Which acquisition channels drive long-term value?
- How much revenue is lost due to customer churn?

## Key Insights
- Basic plan customers exhibit the highest churn rates.
- Referral-acquired customers show the strongest retention and lifetime value.
- Early-stage churn (≤6 months) contributes disproportionately to revenue loss.

## Repository Structure
- `/data` – Raw dataset
